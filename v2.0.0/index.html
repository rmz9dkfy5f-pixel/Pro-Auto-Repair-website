FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers before you call — though we're always happy to talk through your specific situation.

Do I need an appointment?
Appointments help us prepare for your visit and get you in faster. Walk-ins are welcome, but calling ahead is the best way to make sure we have time set aside for you. Call (919) 803-5253.
What vehicles do you work on?
We service most domestic and import makes and models, including Toyota, Honda, Ford, GM, BMW, Mercedes, Audi, and Lexus. Call to confirm if you have a specialty, diesel, or electric vehicle.
How do I know what to approve before work starts?
We give you a written estimate before we begin any work. If we find something unexpected during the job, we call you before proceeding. You're never on the hook for work you didn't approve.
Do you work with extended warranties?
In many cases, yes. Bring your warranty documentation and we'll review what's covered before we start work.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit and debit cards, cash, and most app-based payments. Ask about financing options when you call for larger repairs.
